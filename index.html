Meggie Marschner

University of Mannheim.

Hi!

I am Meggie and I am a PhD candidate in Mathematical Optimization at the University of Mannheim, working on hierarchical variational inequalities and equilibrium problems.

I hold a Master’s degree in Mathematics from the Georg-August-University Göttingen, where I also did my Bachelor degree (2-subject bachelor Mathematics and Economics).

Research Interests: monotone operators, bilevel optimization, hierarchical optimization, (stochastic) variational inequalities, game theory.
